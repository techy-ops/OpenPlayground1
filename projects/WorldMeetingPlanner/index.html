How to Use World Meeting Planner

Step 1: Add Cities

Search and add cities where your team members are located. You can add unlimited cities.

Step 2: Use the Timeline

Drag the slider to see corresponding times across all timezones. The "Golden Hour" shows when everyone is likely available (9 AM - 5 PM local).

Step 3: Generate Invites

Set your meeting details and copy formatted invite text for all participants.
