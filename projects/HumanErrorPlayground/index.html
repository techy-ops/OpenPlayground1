What We're Learning

Button Ambiguity

When primary and secondary actions look too similar, users often click the wrong button. Clear visual hierarchy reduces errors by 40%.

Form Validation Timing

Real-time validation catches 60% more errors than validation on submission. However, too much validation can be overwhelming.

Design Principles

Clear Hierarchy

Use size, color, and placement to show importance

Clear Labels

Action verbs work better than vague terms

Progressive Disclosure

Show options only when needed to reduce overwhelm

Forgiveness

Make actions reversible to reduce error anxiety
